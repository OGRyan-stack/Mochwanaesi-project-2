Who Can Apply?

Our programs are designed for motivated students who meet the following criteria:

  • Currently enrolled in high school or university in South Africa
  • Demonstrated commitment to academic excellence and personal growth
  • From an underserved community or facing financial barriers to education
  • Willing to actively participate in program activities and workshops
  • Able to provide academic transcripts and a letter of recommendation

Don't meet all criteria? We encourage you to apply anyway. We review each application holistically and consider individual circumstances.
