Our Story

Founded in 2013, Mochwanaesi Foundation was born from a simple yet powerful belief: every young person deserves access to quality education and the support they need to succeed. What started as a small mentorship program with just 50 students has grown into a comprehensive educational initiative serving over 500 students annually across South Africa.

Our journey began when a group of educators and community leaders recognized the significant barriers facing talented youth in underserved communities. They saw students with incredible potential who lacked access to resources, guidance, and opportunities. Determined to make a difference, they established our foundation to bridge this gap.

Over the past decade, we've expanded our programs to include academic support, career guidance, and scholarship opportunities. We've built partnerships with schools, universities, and businesses to create pathways to success for our students. Today, we're proud to have helped hundreds of young people achieve their educational goals and build brighter futures for themselves and their communities.

Our Mission

To empower South African youth from underserved communities by providing comprehensive educational support, mentorship, and resources that enable them to achieve academic excellence, develop essential life skills, and become leaders who create positive change in their communities.

Our Vision

A South Africa where every young person, regardless of their background or circumstances, has access to quality education and the support they need to unlock their full potential and contribute meaningfully to society.
